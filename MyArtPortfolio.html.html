Artist Statement

I am an aspiring artist, currently studying in University of the Cordilleras, I do arts and animations as my part time work or hobby since I am at elementary school. I love making artworks like portraits, charcoal drawings, animating, graphic designing, editing, and many more. I was at early age when I discovered that I am good at drawing or arts, I participate in some competitions in my past schools and won some awards, you can rest assured that I'm good on what I do. In addition, I give my best effort in this workplace.

I am inspired to make art by the concept of exploration, which means I wanted to make new things or things that I could improve upon. Seeing others produce amazing works of art makes me want to work even harder and improve at everything I do. I believe that different artists use different materials based on how they produce their work and express themselves. After considering all my experiences as an artist, I believe that people's differing viewpointss do have an impact on art itself. For this reason, I express myself through my artwork and allow audiences to understand and perceive my story.
